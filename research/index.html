Research

My research interests focus on statistical and data science methods, particularly time series, spatial, and spatiotemporal analysis, and their applications to real-world problems in physics, economics, environmental sciences, and health sciences. I am also open to exploring new theoretical and applied areas.

Applications
The use of statistical and machine learning methods to analyze data, draw meaningful conclusions, and make informed decisions in diverse areas of applications.
Jul 1, 2023
Spatio-temporal Analysis
Statistical methods that analyze and interpret data that is collected not only over a period of time but also across a spatial domain. This involves analyzing spatial and temporal patterns and changes in the data over both space and time.
Jul 1, 2023
Time Series Analysis
An area of statistics that analyzes and interprets data collected or recorded over a period of time, in order to extract meaningful characteristics in time and forecast future values.
